## Policy

Research and technical claims should cite:

```text
primary papers
official docs
official repos/model cards
measured project outputs
project-authored notes marked as internal drafts
```

Do not cite:

```text
Claude
ChatGPT
assistant output
drafting-model output
unsourced AI-generated summaries
```
