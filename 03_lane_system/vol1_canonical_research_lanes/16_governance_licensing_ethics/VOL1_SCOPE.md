# Scope — Governance, Licensing, Ethics, and Responsible Use

## In scope
- model licenses
- dataset licenses
- commercial-use restrictions
- synthetic content disclosure
- face/voice consent
- data provenance
- model cards
- data cards
- risk registers
- incident disclosure
- human oversight
- audit logs

## Out of scope / caution
- legal advice
- policy certainty where laws vary by jurisdiction
- moralizing instead of operational guidance

## Vol. 1 target
This lane should give a small or mid-sized assistant enough domain scaffolding to answer practitioner-level questions, route to better source material, and identify when current web verification or expert review is required.
