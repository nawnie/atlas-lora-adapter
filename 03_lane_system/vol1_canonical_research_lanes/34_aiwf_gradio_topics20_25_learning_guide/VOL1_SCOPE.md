# Scope

In scope:

- Gradio as the interactive learning and application surface for AIWF Topics 20–25.
- Cross-topic labs and capstones that combine Gradio, lakeFS, document AI, synthetic data, multi-agent architecture, and cost planning.
- Machine routing from learning-guide sections into canonical lanes.
- Diagram/capstone/glossary material that helps an AI assistant plan lessons, projects, and implementations.

Out of scope:

- Replacing the canonical topic lanes.
- Treating draft page counts or model rankings as verified facts without source checks.
- Generating the final PDF or diagrams unless explicitly requested.
