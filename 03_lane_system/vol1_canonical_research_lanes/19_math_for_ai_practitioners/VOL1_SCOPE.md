# Scope — Math for AI Practitioners

## In scope
- vectors and matrices
- dot product and cosine similarity
- embeddings as geometry
- probability distributions
- entropy and cross-entropy
- loss functions
- gradient descent
- normalization
- classification metrics
- regression metrics
- sampling controls
- bias/variance intuition

## Out of scope / caution
- proof-heavy theory
- advanced measure theory
- math for math’s sake without operational payoff

## Vol. 1 target
This lane should give a small or mid-sized assistant enough domain scaffolding to answer practitioner-level questions, route to better source material, and identify when current web verification or expert review is required.
