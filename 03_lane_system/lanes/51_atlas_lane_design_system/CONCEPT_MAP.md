# Concept Map — Atlas Lane Design System

## Core concepts

- Lanes are routing destinations.
- Boundaries prevent retrieval confusion.
- Lane kits make lanes testable.
- Lane quality matters more than lane count.

- Decide whether an idea becomes a lane or a card.
- Validate lane readiness.
- Merge, split, or clarify lanes.
